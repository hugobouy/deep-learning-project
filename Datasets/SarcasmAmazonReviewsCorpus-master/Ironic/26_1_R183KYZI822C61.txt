<TITLE>Sophie has no spots now</TITLE>
<DATE>September 20, 2009</DATE>
<AUTHOR>Bronwyn A. Shields</AUTHOR>
<PRODUCT>Vulli Sophie the Giraffe Teether, Brown/ White (Baby Product)</PRODUCT>
<REVIEW>
This toy is WAY over rated.  The spots rub off with water. Sophie is naked now and her eyes look like her mascara has run down her face.  Not to mention that she feels and sounds like a dog toy, which is why my mom's dog ate her head.
