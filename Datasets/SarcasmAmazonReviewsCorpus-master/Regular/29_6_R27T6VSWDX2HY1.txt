<TITLE>Keurig Green Tea is good.</TITLE>
<DATE>December 23, 2009</DATE>
<AUTHOR>Kathryn L. Moss</AUTHOR>
<PRODUCT>Bigelow Tea Organic Green Tea K-Cup For Keurig Brewers, 24-Count,  Boxes (Pack of 2) (Grocery)</PRODUCT>
<REVIEW>
This makes a good cup of green tea. The mild flavor is refreshing and not too bitter. I typically make this as a hot beverage, but it is also very good cold. I prefer this tea unsweetened.
</REVIEW>
